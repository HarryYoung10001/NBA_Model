\section{$RAPM_{team}: Predictor\ of\ Win\%$}

Team RAPM is calculated as:

\begin{equation}
\text{RAPM}_{\text{team}} = \frac{\sum_{i\in team} \text{RAPM}_i \times \text{Minutes}_i}{\sum_{i\in team} \text{Minutes}_i}
\end{equation}

Based on NBA regular season data from 2015 to 2023, comprising 270 team-season observations, we get:

\begin{equation}
\widehat{\text{Win\%}} = 0.5120 + 0.1287 \times \text{RAPM} ,R^2 = 0.8170
\end{equation}
